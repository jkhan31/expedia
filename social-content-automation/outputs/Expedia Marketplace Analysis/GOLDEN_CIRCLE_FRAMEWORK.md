# Expedia Marketplace Analysis: Golden Circle Framework

## The Golden Circle Applied to This Project

### WHY (Core Insight)
**Most people assume price drives luxury hotel booking gaps. But data reveals it's visibility.**

The principle: When you measure what actually happens (not what you assume), you discover different priorities.

### HOW (The Mechanism)
**Marketplace algorithms prioritize conversion. For luxury segment, visibility directly impacts booking probability.**

When search algorithms rank results:
- Budget hotels: High visibility + High conversion = Top ranking
- Luxury hotels: High conversion + Low visibility = Buried in results

Result: Searchers never consider luxury options because they don't see them.

### WHAT (The Evidence)
**Luxury hotels: 2.20% booking rate | Budget hotels: 3.04% booking rate | Gap: 27%**

The gap isn't demand destruction (luxury is still booked). It's visibility collapse (luxury isn't shown).

## Rule of Thumb

**For EVERY carousel, EVERY platform:**

1. Lead with WHY (the insight, the surprise, the reason to care)
2. Explain HOW (the mechanism, the principle)
3. Back with WHAT (the data, the proof)

Same circle. Different emphasis per platform.

Same data. Different hooks per post.

Same insight. Different language per audience.

That's how you get maximum reach while maintaining consistent message.
